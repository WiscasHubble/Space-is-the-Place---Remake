Space Models and Spaceflight History

I have been a collector and builder of space models since the 1960s. As a modeler I think "my grasp exceeds my reach" often - my models are never as beautifully finished as others I see at model shows - but I am comfortable with this. I get the greatest thrill out of conceiving of something a bit unusal, "pushing the envelope" a bit, and then seeing it realized. I never seem to have the patience, let alone the skills, to do the super-detailing and beautiful finishes of some other modelers. But I do have a knack for taking on some unusual subjects, as I think you'll see in the following pages. In choosing subjects I am interested in capturing significant moments or honouring technical achievements in the history of spaceflight, as well as subjects of interest for personal reasons. In the following pages I have tried to provide some historical context relating to each of the subjects being modeled.
